Why do I need an app to run my garden?

You do not necessarily need an app for it, however app can track your plant and store data. By using this data you can grow healthier plants with less effort. Through the app you can connect with a community of gardeners near you, keep a photo journal and get reminders pruning, or advice on the best spots for growth.

John P.

Where can I find instructions for this system?

Instructions on how to launch the system and use the app can be found in the ‘Help’ section of the main menu.

Alex D.

What kind of soil should I use?

You can use soil from your yard and add a lot of compost or buy a topsoil. We recommend buying a topsoil mixed with compost and peat moss, which can also be called a “triple mix”.

Carter G.

I am new to gardening and not sure where to start. What do I do?

In the app you can start with a Planning Section which will guide you through the whole process of choosing the right plant for your needs and building you a care calendar.
