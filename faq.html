Why do I need an app to run my garden?

You do not necessarily need an app for it, however app can track your plant and store data. By using this data you can grow healthier plants with less effort. Through the app you can connect with a community of gardeners near you, keep a photo journal and get reminders pruning, or advice on the best spots for growth.
